ALL ABOUT ME

Matthew Arenn C Mamangon

Welcome To DewyTenders! Where we are passionate about bringing you the ultimate chicken tender experience. Our mission is to serve up fresh, crispy chicken tenders paired with a variety of delicious dips that cater to every taste. DewyTenders started with a simple idea to create the perfect tender that’s juicy on the inside and crispy on the outside. That will make you want to pair it with rice! We use high quality ingredients and hand bread our tenders daily to ensure every bite is packed with flavor. Whether you’re craving a classic taste or something a bit more adventurous, We have the perfect dip to complement your meal. At DewyTenders, We believe that great food brings people together, and we’re excited to be a part of your next gathering, lunch, or late night snack.

Why Choose DewyTenders?

  • Fresh and Crispy: Hand breaded tenders made to order. Variety of Dips: From zesty ranch to sweet honey mustard, explore our delicious dip options! Order Online: Quick and easy delivery to your door.
